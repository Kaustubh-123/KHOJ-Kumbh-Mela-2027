Team 21 Presents

KHOJ

AI-Powered Emergency Triage & Missing Person Network for Kumbh Mela 2027

The Challenge

100M+

Pilgrims Gathered

The largest human gathering on Earth. When a child goes missing, traditional police reporting is too slow and centralized.

⚠️

Facial Recognition Fails

Extreme occlusions, poor angles, and processing millions of faces is impossible in real-time.

🔒

Privacy Concerns

Mass surveillance of biometric data violates fundamental privacy rights of attendees.

The Solution: Edge Nodes

Interactive Kiosks

Lightning-fast wizard UI designed to lower cognitive load for panicked parents reporting missing children.

Passive Vector Search

Cameras extract clothing color vectors and transit metadata—completely ignoring biometric facial data.

Real Data Integration

Integrated directly with Nashik KML datasets to route police units instantly via Haversine distances.
